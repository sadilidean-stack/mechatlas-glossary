Actuators

Devices that convert electrical signals into physical movement; used to move or control mechanical systems (motors, cylinders, robotic arms).

Analog Input/Output Modules (4–20mA, 0–10V)

Modules that bridge analog sensors and digital systems using A/D and D/A conversion; common ranges: 4–20mA, 0–10V.

Arduino

Microcontroller platform for prototyping and embedded projects; reads sensors and drives actuators.

Automatic Valve Control

Valves actuated by solenoids, pilots or sensors to automate flow control without human intervention.

Belt Drive

Mechanical system using belts to transmit power between rotating shafts; smooth and flexible transmission.

Blue LED

LED emitting in the 450–495 nm range used in displays and RGB lighting.

Breadboard

Solderless prototyping board for building and testing circuits quickly.

Bridge Rectifier Module

Four-diode bridge used for full-wave rectification to convert AC to DC in power supplies.

CAN Interface Module

Enables communication over Controller Area Network (CAN) for distributed device networking.

Capacitor

Stores and releases electrical energy; used for filtering, smoothing, timing and transient suppression.

Carbon Film Resistor

Fixed resistor type made by depositing carbon film; common, inexpensive general-purpose resistor.

Carbon Zinc Dry Cell Batteries

Primary cells used in low-cost portable devices; non-rechargeable.

Chain

Linked metal segments used to transmit rotary motion between sprockets with minimal slip.

CNC Machines

Computer-controlled machines for precise subtractive manufacturing tasks.

Coil

Wound conductor generating magnetic field when current flows; used in relays, inductors and motors.

Computer / Laptop

Used to program controllers, log data and analyze experiments.

Control Relay

Electrically operated switch that uses a low-power input to switch higher-power circuits.

Control Valves

Final control elements that modulate flow, pressure or level based on controller signals.

Conveyor Systems

Mechanical systems to transport materials along a fixed path in manufacturing and logistics.

Copper Bars

Conductive busbars used for power distribution inside panels and enclosures.

Copper Wire

Common conductor for electrical wiring due to high conductivity and ductility.

Coupling Shaft

Connects motor shaft to driven load, transferring torque and compensating slight misalignment.

CPU Module (Processor)

Processing unit in PLCs/controllers that executes logic and coordinates I/O.

Data Logger

Device that records sensor data over time for analysis and troubleshooting.

DHT11 Sensor

Basic temperature and humidity sensor for simple monitoring tasks.

Digital Input Module

Monitors discrete ON/OFF signals from switches and sensors to the controller.

Digital Ohmmeter

Measures resistance digitally for component testing and troubleshooting.

Digital Output Module (relay, transistor, triac)

Sends binary signals from controllers to actuators; types include relay (mechanical), transistor (DC) and triac (AC).

Double Solenoid 5/2 Valve

Pneumatic valve with two solenoids and five ports used to control double-acting cylinders.

Driven Gear

Gear that receives motion from the driving gear and changes speed or torque according to ratio.

Driving Gear

Input gear that transmits motion and torque into a gear train.

Electro Pneumatic Converters

Convert electrical controller signals (4–20mA or 0–10V) into proportional pneumatic pressure for actuators.

Electrolytes

Substances that produce ions when dissolved; essential for batteries and electrochemical processes.

Electrical Terminal Block

Provides secure, organized connections for multiple wires in panels and enclosures.

Electrical Timer Base Socket

Mounting base that holds and connects plug-in electronic timers for easy replacement and wiring.

Electronic Timer

Provides timed ON/OFF control for relays and solenoids in automated sequences.

Emergency Stop Switches

Fail-safe switches that immediately remove power to machinery to protect operators.

Ethernet Switch

Network device connecting multiple Ethernet devices and forwarding data packets in LANs.

Feedback Encoder

Provides position/speed feedback from motor shafts to controllers for closed-loop control.

Fieldbus Cable

Cable used to connect devices on fieldbus networks, enabling distributed device communication.

Flow Control Pumps

Pumps whose output can be adjusted by controllers to regulate flow rate and pressure in processes.

Flowchart Diagramming Tool

Software used to visualize control logic and process flow for design and troubleshooting.

Forklifts

Industrial trucks used to lift and move heavy loads in warehouses and factories.

Fossil Fuels

Coal, oil and natural gas used as energy sources for power and transportation.

Full Joint

Kinematic connection providing full intended relative motion between links.

Generator

Converts mechanical energy into electrical energy for supply or testing purposes.

Grinder

Machine tool for abrasion, finishing and material removal to tight tolerances.

Green LED

LED emitting in the 495–570 nm range used for status indicators and signaling.

Hot-Wire Ammeter

Measures current by detecting thermal expansion of a wire; useful for high-frequency measurements.

Hydraulic Turbines

Convert water flow energy to mechanical energy for electricity generation.

Inductive Proximity Sensor

Detects metal objects using electromagnetic fields; used for position and presence sensing.

Indicators / Gauges

Display instruments for pressure, level, temperature and other process variables.

Industrial Router

Rugged networking device for industrial environments enabling protocol bridging and remote access.

Jumper Wire

Short insulated wires for connecting points on breadboards and prototypes.

Lead‑Acid Batteries

Rechargeable batteries used for vehicle starting, backup power and industrial applications.

LED Holder

Socket or clip securing LEDs and protecting leads while allowing easy replacement.

Light Emitting Diode (LED)

Semiconductor that emits light when forward-biased; used for indicators and lighting.

Limit Switch Sensor

Mechanical switch that detects physical movement when an actuator reaches a position. Provides positional feedback to timers or relays.

Limit Switches

Switch operated by motion of a machine part or presence of an object; used to detect mechanical movement or position.

Lithium Batteries

High‑energy, lightweight batteries used in electronics, EVs and renewable systems for portable energy storage.

Machine

Device that converts input energy into useful mechanical work through mechanisms and actuators.

Magnets

Materials producing magnetic fields; used in motors, generators, sensors and data storage devices.

Manual (Valve)

Valve operated directly by a human via lever or knob to start, stop or change fluid flow; used for simple/manual control.

Mercury Oxide Batteries

Primary cells providing stable voltage for precision equipment; largely replaced due to environmental concerns.

Microcontroller Demo Board

Small board with a microcontroller used to demonstrate programming, interfacing and embedded concepts.

Motor Driver Module (L298N)

Module that controls DC/stepper motors, allowing microcontrollers to drive higher-current motors safely.

Multimeter

Instrument for measuring voltage, current and resistance; essential for testing and troubleshooting circuits.

Oscilloscope

Instrument that displays voltage waveforms over time to analyze signal shape, timing and behavior.

Pneumatic Cylinder

Device converting compressed air into linear motion for extension and retraction in automation systems.

Positioners for Valves & Dampers

Auxiliary devices that ensure actuators reach the correct physical position corresponding to controller output using local feedback.

Potentiometer

Adjustable resistor used to vary voltage or signal levels for tuning, calibration or user input.

Power Adapter

Device that converts mains AC to the required DC voltage/current for controllers, servos or electronics.

Power Supply Module (24V DC, 120/240V AC)

Converts AC mains or other inputs into regulated DC voltages to power CPU, I/O and peripherals in control systems.

Power Supply Unit

Regulated supply that provides required energy for drivers and motors, sized to match voltage/current needs of the load.

Pressure Compensation

Techniques or components used to keep system pressure stable under varying load or demand to protect performance and safety.

Pressure Gauge

Instrument that measures and indicates fluid pressure in hydraulic or pneumatic systems for monitoring and safety.

Pressure Regulator

Valve that reduces a variable input pressure to a stable, lower output pressure to protect downstream equipment and ensure consistent operation.

Pressure Transducer

Sensor that converts fluid pressure into an electrical signal (voltage/current) for monitoring, control and data logging.

Pressure Relief Valve

Safety valve that opens when pressure exceeds a preset limit to divert excess fluid and protect the system from overpressure.

Projector / Computer

Devices used for presentations and for programming/monitoring controllers and experiments.

Proximity Sensor

Non‑contact sensor detecting presence or absence of objects using inductive, capacitive, ultrasonic or photoelectric principles.

Pulse Generator

Device that generates electrical pulses (and direction signals) to drive stepper drivers, controlling steps and speed of stepper motors.

Push Buttons (NO/NC)

Momentary switches: NO closes when pressed, NC opens when pressed; used for start, stop and reset functions.

Raw Materials (RAM)

Basic substances (metals, plastics, wood) used as inputs in manufacturing processes.

Red LED

LED emitting in the red wavelength (≈620–750 nm), commonly used as indicators and signals.

Reed Switch

Magnetically operated sealed switch used to detect piston movement and for discrete position sensing.

Relay Module

Electrically operated switch controlled by low-voltage signals to switch higher-power devices safely.

Resistor

Component that limits or regulates current; used for protection, voltage division and biasing.

Resistor Kit

Assortment of resistors of various values useful for prototyping and learning.

Robotic Arms

Programmable mechanical arms performing tasks like welding, assembly and material handling in automation.

RS485 Converter

Device converting RS‑485 serial to Ethernet or other protocols to integrate legacy devices with modern networks.

Sample Embedded Devices

Pre‑built microcontroller systems used for study and demonstration of embedded functionality.

Safety Pressure Limits

Maximum allowable pressures enforced by relief valves or regulators to protect equipment and personnel.

Screw Pair

Kinematic pair converting rotary into linear motion; used in lead screws and actuators.

Selector Switches

Multi‑position manual switches used to select modes (Auto/Manual/Jog) or redirect circuits.

Sensor Indicator Module

Module with LEDs showing sensor ON/OFF status to aid testing and troubleshooting.

Sensor Mounting Bracket

Bracket for securing sensors with correct alignment to ensure detection accuracy.

Sensors

Devices that detect physical parameters (temperature, distance, light) and provide signals to controllers.

Sequence Valve

Valve that coordinates operations so one actuator acts only after a prior function completes or specified pressure is reached.

Servo Controller

Drive unit that powers a servo motor, interprets commands and uses feedback to achieve precise motion control.

Servo Motor

Motor used in closed‑loop systems to provide precise position, speed or torque under controller command.

Sheave

Pulley wheel used in belt or rope drives to guide belts and transmit power efficiently.

Shielded Cable

Cable with conductive shield to block EMI and preserve signal integrity for sensitive/analog wiring.

Signal Cable Set

Wiring assembly connecting controller, motor, encoder and power to transmit commands and feedback.

Signal Conditioner

Module that amplifies, filters or converts sensor outputs into standardized signals (0–10V, 4–20mA) for controllers.

Silicon

Semiconductor material forming the substrate for microchips; doped to create regions for transistors and logic circuits.

Solar Panel

Device converting sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells for renewable power.

Solenoid Valve

Electrically controlled valve that directs airflow for pneumatic cylinder extension/retraction in automated circuits.

Spherical Pair

Kinematic joint allowing rotation in all directions (ball-and-socket), providing three degrees of freedom.

Start Push Button

Momentary switch used to energize circuits or start machines by completing the circuit while pressed.

Stepper Driver Module

Module that receives pulse+direction signals and sequences currents to stepper motor coils to produce controlled steps.

Stepper Motor

Motor that converts discrete pulses into precise incremental rotations; used for accurate positioning tasks.

Switch

Device that opens or closes an electrical circuit to control power or signals.

Temperature Sensor

Device measuring temperature and converting it to an electrical signal for monitoring and control.

Terminal

Connection point for wires or components providing secure and reliable electrical connections.

Timer & Clock Demo Circuits

Circuits demonstrating timing functions, pulse generation and delay behavior for educational purposes.

Transistors

Semiconductor switches used for amplification and switching; fundamental building blocks of digital logic and electronics.

Voltmeter

Instrument for measuring electric potential difference between two points.

Wattmeter

Instrument that measures real electrical power consumed by a circuit using combined voltage and current sensing.

Welding Equipment

Tools and machines used to join materials by heat or pressure for manufacturing and repair.

Wire Leads

Insulated conductors used to make electrical connections on breadboards or PCBs during prototyping.

Wiring Tools

Tools such as wire strippers, cutters and crimpers used for building and maintaining electrical connections safely.

Zener Diode

Diode designed to allow reverse conduction at a specified voltage for voltage regulation and protection circuits.

1N4007 Rectifier Diode

General-purpose rectifier diode (≈1A, up to 1000V reverse) used in power supplies for AC→DC conversion.

24V DC Power Supply

Provides stable 24V DC commonly used to power sensors, relays and PLC I/O modules.

3/2 Directional Control Valve

Three-port, two-position valve used to supply or vent single-acting actuators.

3D Printers

Additive manufacturing machines that build parts layer-by-layer from digital models.

4/2 Directional Control Valve

Four-port, two-position valve used with double-acting actuators to alternate supply and exhaust.

4/3 Directional Control Valve

Four-port, three-position valve providing extend, retract and neutral/hold states for actuators.

5/2 Directional Control Valve

Five-port, two-position valve commonly used to control double-acting pneumatic cylinders with separate exhausts.

7400 IC (NAND)

TTL IC with four independent 2-input NAND gates in a 14-pin DIP package; fundamental logic building block.

7402 IC (NOR)

TTL IC implementing NOR gates; output HIGH only when all inputs are LOW.

7404 IC (NOT Gate)

TTL IC containing six independent inverters (NOT gates); outputs the logical complement of inputs.

7408 IC (AND Gate)

TTL IC with four 2‑input AND gates; output HIGH only when both inputs are HIGH.

7432 IC (OR Gate)

TTL IC containing four 2‑input OR gates; output HIGH if at least one input is HIGH.
